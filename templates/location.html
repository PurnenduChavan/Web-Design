Discover the best places to visit in Pune

Called “Queen of the Deccan,” Pune is the cultural capital of the Maratha peoples. The city first gained importance as the capital of the Marathas in the 17th century. It was temporarily captured by the Mughals but again served as the official Maratha capital from 1714 until its fall to the British in 1817.

Explore the magnificent forts, beautiful monuments, buzzing bazaars and quaint streets that will transport you back to a grander era long since past.
